Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary for medical travel facilitation, including:

  • Identity Data (Name, Passport Number, Age).
  • Contact Data (Phone, Email, Address).
  • Health Data (Medical Reports, X-Rays, Prescriptions).
  • Transaction Data (Payment IDs, Booking History).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for:

  • Facilitating medical consultations with partner hospitals.
  • Issuing Visa Invitation Letters.
  • Arranging logistics (Airport pickup, Accommodation).

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your health data. Medical records are stored in secure lockers and shared only with the specific doctors/hospitals you choose.

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We may share your data with:

  • Hospitals and Doctors (for treatment planning).
  • Government Authorities (for Visa processing, if required).

We do NOT sell your data to advertisers.

5. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: support@treatint.com
